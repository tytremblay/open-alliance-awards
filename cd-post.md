# 🏆 The Open Alliance Awards

![trophy|320x320](upload://trophy.png)

The Open Alliance is one of the best things about this community — a whole season
of teams building in the open, showing their work, and helping each other get
better. So I built a little awards ceremony for it.

It reads a season of public Open Alliance build threads and hands out awards.
Some are decided by the numbers (likes, views, posts), some are judged on the
writing and engineering in the threads themselves. Every winner gets a black-tie
write-up and a trophy. A team can take home **at most one award all night**, so
the spotlight spreads as wide as possible across the community.

**🎬 See this season's ceremony → https://tytremblay.github.io/open-alliance-awards**

---

## The Marquee Awards
The headliners — the threads the whole community rallied around.

- **🏆 Best Picture** — The build thread the community loved most, by total likes.
- **👀 Audience Award** — A build thread the whole community kept coming back to.
- **💬 Most Talkative Ensemble** — The thread that simply would not stop talking, by post count.
- **❤️ People's Choice** — One of the most-liked single posts of the whole season.

## The Spotlight Awards
Relative and quality-based, so the smaller and quieter threads get their moment.

- **🌱 Rookie / Newcomer Spotlight** — The standout build log from a newer team.
- **💎 Hidden Gem** — A thread the crowd hasn't found yet — lots of love, modest traffic.
- **🎯 Quality Over Quantity** — Highest average likes per post; every post landed.
- **🚀 Best Opening Night** — The strongest debut: the most-liked opening post of the season.
- **🌍 Around the World** — Celebrating the global reach of the Open Alliance.

## The Juried Awards
These ones are read and judged on the substance of the thread, not the stats.

- **✍️ Best Original Screenplay** — The best writing and storytelling in a build log.
- **🔧 Best Engineering Deep-Dive** — The most impressive technical breakdown of the season.
- **🎥 Best Cinematography** — The best use of photos, CAD renders, and video.
- **😂 Best Comedic Moment** — The funniest, most good-natured moment of the season.
- **🦾 Best Comeback** — The best recovery-from-disaster arc.
- **🤝 Best Supporting Team** — The team that gave most generously back to the community.
